Our Story

Built by AI Obsessives

We started AI-Solutions with one belief — that every business, regardless of size, deserves access to world-class artificial intelligence.

Our Mission

Making AI Accessible to All

AI-Solutions was founded in Sunderland, UK with a simple mission: to democratise artificial intelligence for businesses of every size. We believe the gap between enterprise AI capabilities and SME access is a problem worth solving.

From our first chatbot deployment to serving 500+ clients globally, we've stayed true to our founding principle — build AI that actually works for real people, in real businesses, with measurable results.

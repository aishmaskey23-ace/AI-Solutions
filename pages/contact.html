FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical AI project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A chatbot or virtual assistant typically takes 4–8 weeks. Full AI analytics platforms or automation systems range from 8–16 weeks. Our prototyping service delivers a working MVP in as little as 2 weeks.

Do I need technical knowledge to work with you?

Not at all. We work with business leaders, not just engineers. We translate your business goals into technical solutions and keep you informed with clear, jargon-free communication throughout.

What industries do you work with?

We've delivered AI solutions across FinTech, HealthTech, EdTech, Retail, SaaS, HR, and more. Our technology is industry-agnostic — if you have a workflow or data challenge, we can help.

How is pricing structured?

Pricing is project-based and tailored to your specific needs. We provide a detailed quote after a discovery call. We offer both fixed-price projects and retainer-based ongoing support arrangements.

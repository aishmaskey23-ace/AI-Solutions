FAQ

Pricing Questions Answered

Can I switch plans at any time?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing date. No lock-in contracts on monthly plans.

What happens after my 14-day trial?

You won't be charged automatically. At the end of your trial, you choose which plan to continue with. We'll send you a reminder 3 days before the trial ends.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or education?

Yes — we offer 30% off for verified nonprofits, charities, and educational institutions. Contact us with your organisation's details to apply.

What's included in setup and onboarding?

All plans include a guided setup session. Professional and Enterprise plans include a dedicated onboarding call, workflow mapping, and integration support to get you live as fast as possible.
